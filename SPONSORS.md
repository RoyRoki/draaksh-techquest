## 📋 Outreach Tips

- **Local companies (Cyber Help, Technogleam, Softech):** Call first, email after. They respond to direct contact faster.
- **GDG Siliguri:** Don't ask for money first — propose co-organizer/community partner role. Gets you promotion + legitimacy + in-kind support.
- **EdTech platforms (GFG, CodeChef, HackerEarth):** They often give in-kind (subscriptions, certificates, swag) even without cash — still valuable for prize pool.
- **STPI Siliguri:** Frame as nurturing North Bengal's IT talent pipeline — government-aligned language works.
- **NASSCOM Foundation:** Lead with social impact — digital skilling, tier-2 city tech ecosystem.
- **Always attach** a 1-page sponsor deck PDF with: audience demographics, reach, tiers, logo placement, social mentions.
